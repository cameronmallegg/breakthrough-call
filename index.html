Before we get on, you'll fill out a short intake form. I read every word of it before we speak. We don't waste time on surface level discovery — we go straight to the work.

First 30 minutes

We go deep on where you actually are. What's working, what isn't, and what you've been avoiding being honest about.

Next 30 minutes

We identify the specific beliefs and patterns that have been running your life without your permission. You'll see them clearly, probably for the first time.

Final 30 minutes

We build a concrete direction and first steps. Not a vision board. A real plan you can execute starting the same day.

You walk away knowing exactly what has been holding you back and exactly what to do about it. That's the only outcome I'm interested in.

After every call you'll receive a written summary of everything we covered — your key insights, the beliefs we identified, and your exact action plan. Formatted cleanly so you can reference it any time you need a reminder of where you're going and why.
